Omelette

Recipe for an omelette

This simple recipe will make you loved amongst your friends

Things you need

  • Eggs
  • Preferred meat
  • Preferred seasoning
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl
  2. Add meat and seasoning to the bowl
  3. Mix the bowl with a forg or mixer till the eggs are mixed
  4. Heat up the pan and pour the content of the bowl onto the pan
  5. When the omelettes edges look cooked add cheese onto omelette and lift one edge to the other to create a pocket
  6. Cook the omelette a bit more to let the cheese melt and pocket edges to close
  7. Serve and enjoy
