Creamy pasta

Recipe for creamy pasta dish

This simple recipe will make you loved amongst your friends

Things you need

  • Pasta
  • Minced meat
  • Preferred seasoning
  • Cheese
  • Cooking cream
  • Dill

Steps

  1. Boil the water and add pasta
  2. Start cooking your minced meat until it's nice and brown but don't let pasta overcook
  3. When minced meat is brown, season it and add cooking cream
  4. Bring the cream to a slight boil and add the content to the pasta
  5. Add a bit of dill, mix the pasta and the cream and let it set for a minute
  6. Serve the pasta and enjoy
