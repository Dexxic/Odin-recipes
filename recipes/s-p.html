Salty pancakes

Recipe for minced meat pancakes

This simple recipe will make you loved amongst your friends

Things you need

  • Pancake flour and water
  • Minced meat
  • Preferred seasoning
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the pancake flour and set it aside to set
  2. Start cooking your minced meat until it's nice and brown
  3. Lay out the pancake and place some minced meat in the middle, add cheese
  4. Fold the edges of the pancake towards the middle and flip the created pocket over
  5. Toss the tray full of pockets into the oven to heat up the pocket and let the cheese in the middle melt
  6. Take out the pockets and enjoy
